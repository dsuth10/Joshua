What is the Content Library?

The Content Library is a read-only space for students where you share course materials, resources, and references. Students can view and copy content but cannot edit the originals.

Best Practices:

  • Organize by units, topics, or weeks
  • Use clear, descriptive section and page names
  • Include multimedia (videos, images, audio)
  • Update regularly with new materials

What are Student Sections?

Each student gets a private notebook section visible only to you and them. Perfect for assignments, personal notes, and one-on-one feedback.
